What's inside

Built for people who
already know the lifts

No streak confetti, no AI coach nagging you. Just the tools a self-directed athlete actually uses, sharpened until they disappear.

01

Phase-aware logging

Your log knows the block you're in — accumulation, intensification, peak. Editable templates, set adjustments that follow the program instead of fighting it.

02

Nutrition that does the math

Macro targets, real serving sizes, and fiber that scales with the portion. Scan a barcode and the food's already filled in.

barcode → Open Food Facts
03

Statistics you can trust

Trends, not noise. A real regression engine reads through day-to-day scatter so you see whether the line is actually moving.

Theil–Sen · Mann–Kendall · 7-day avg
04

Rest-day advisor

Reads your step count and your run of load days, then tells you plainly when to push and when to back off. Recovery you don't have to guess at.

05

Built on HealthKit

Bodyweight flows in, finished workouts flow out, and your runs and walks land as cardio — without leaving Apple's vault.

06

Private by design

Everything lives on your device. Versioned backups, an atomic restore that can't half-finish, and CSV export whenever you want out.

The progression

You don't unlock tiers.
You forge them.

Consistency is the only currency. Each tier is earned through the work itself — and the metal gets harder to reach the further you go.

Iron
Where everyone starts. Raw, unproven, full of potential.
TIER 01
Steel
Tempered by showing up. The first sign the habit is holding.
TIER 02
Damascus
Forged through repetition until the pattern emerges. Few keep going this long.
TIER 03

The apex metal. Legendary, and earned by almost no one. Proof of the long game.

Aristeia

Local-first, on purpose

Your training
stays yours.

  • No account, ever. Open the app and start logging. Nothing to sign up for, nothing to forget.
  • No cloud, no tracking. Your data never leaves your phone unless you export it yourself.
  • Backups that can't break. Versioned, validated, and restored atomically — with a safety snapshot before anything changes.
  • Export anytime. Full CSV and JSON. It's your log; leave whenever you like.
