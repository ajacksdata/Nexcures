The through-line

A few rules
everything follows.

I'm one person building tools I use myself, every day. That keeps the list of priorities short and honest.

01

Private by default

Your data stays on your device. No accounts to create, nothing harvested, nothing sold. Export it and walk away whenever you like.

02

Measure, don't guess

Real statistics and honest trends over vanity streaks. If a number is noisy, the app says so instead of dressing it up.

03

Opinionated on purpose

Built for people who already take this seriously. Fewer settings, stronger defaults, no hand-holding.

04

Made to be lived with

Tools earn their place by surviving daily use. If I wouldn't reach for it tomorrow morning, it doesn't ship.
